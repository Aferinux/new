About Dr. Victor Adetunji

Dr. Victor Adetunji is a lead facilitator and Forex Expert at Traintotradethemarket. He is an astute professional trader and trainer of repute who has mentored several people over time on trading the financial market profitably.

Presently, he is a financial investment advisor, and as well as providing coaching for aspiring traders via the Traintotradethemarket website and offsite in the training facility in Lagos. Victor makes sure he is personally available to attend to his students on a regurlar basis. He is an extremely passionate person when it comes to trading and life, and this passion carries through in his desire to share knowledge with others.

On a daily basis, Dr. Victor applies his deep skills in the system's integration and design strategy to develop features to help retail traders make profit from the market.

Dr. Victor's success as a trader and coach can be attributed to his unique keep-it-simple trading philosophy. If you are an aspiring forex trader looking for a genuine education, look no further!
